Hi ,

You are a beacon of light so true,
A soul that radiates warmth like morning dew.
In your presence, happiness takes flight,
A guiding star through life's darkest night.

With a smile that brightens the gloomiest day,
Your heart, a flame that never tires.
Your spirit uplifts and inspires,
In your embrace, troubles find relent.

You bring comfort and joy in every way.
Your laughter, like music, lifts spirits high,
With kindness and grace, you touch hearts nigh.
May life's blessings forever shine on your path.

To the anxious soul, with a tender heart,
I see your struggle, though it may be hard.
In a world of worry, where thoughts run deep,
But fear not, dear one, for you are strong,
You navigate a maze, seeking solace to keep.

So, anxious soul, hold on to hope's embrace,
In time, serenity will find its place.
Reach out your hand, let others lend support.
Believe in your resilience, your spirit's might,
And emerge from anxiety's grip into radiant light.

To the weary heart, burdened and blue,
I see the shadows that surround you,
Remember, dear one, that healing has begun.
In moments of despair, when hope feels far,
Know that you are cherished just as you are.
In the depths of darkness, you may reside,
But within you, a flicker of light abides.

So, dear one, hold on to the flame within,
Seek solace in gentle whispers of grace,
Let it guide you through darkness, to begin
Through the storm, you'll find strength anew,
And brighter days will soon break through.
A chapter of healing, of self-discovery,
Where your spirit soars, unburdened and free.

To the empty vessel, longing for more,
I sense the void that you can't ignore.
In the depths of emptiness, you reside,
But within you, dormant passions hide.

Though you may feel hollow, lost and detached,
There's a spark within you, waiting to be matched.
In the silence, listen closely to your soul's plea,
For it holds the key to set you free.

So, dear one, don't despair in this empty space,
Embrace the opportunity to find your grace.
Discover the passions that light your way,
And watch as emptiness gives rise to a brand-new day.
